What You Need to Login

Before accessing your EERO admin panel, ensure you have:

  • Your EERO account email address
  • Your EERO account password
  • The EERO mobile app installed (iOS/Android)
  • Your phone connected to the EERO network
  • Admin privileges for the EERO network
Note: You must be connected to your EERO network to access admin settings. If you've forgotten your password, use the password reset option in the EERO app.

Step-by-Step Login Guide

1

Via EERO Mobile App

Open the EERO app on your smartphone. Tap "Sign In" and enter your EERO account credentials. Ensure your phone is connected to your EERO network for full access.

2

Web Browser Access

Visit eero.com/login from any browser. Enter your EERO account email and password. This provides limited access compared to the mobile app.

3

Using Amazon Account

If your EERO is linked to an Amazon account, you can log in using your Amazon credentials through the "Sign in with Amazon" option in the EERO app.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Common Problems and Solutions

  • Incorrect Password: Use password reset in the EERO app or via email
  • App Not Connecting: Ensure Bluetooth is enabled and you're on the EERO network
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Have your phone ready to receive verification codes
  • Amazon Account Issues: Check your Amazon login separately if using Amazon credentials
  • No Admin Privileges: Contact the network owner to be granted admin access
Security Tip: Always download the EERO app from official app stores. EERO will never ask for your password outside of the official app or eero.com website.
